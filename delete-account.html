Delete your CollectIt account

Last updated: 7 July 2026

You can permanently delete your CollectIt account and associated data directly in the App, or request deletion by email if you no longer have access to the App.

1. Delete your account in the App

Recommended self-service path:
  1. Open the CollectIt app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Open Account.
  4. Tap Delete account permanently.
  5. Confirm the deletion prompt.

This is the fastest way to delete your account if you can still sign in.

2. Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the App, email us at henri.irmscher@gmail.com with the subject line Delete my CollectIt account.

Please include:

  • the email address linked to your account;
  • your username, if available;
  • a short statement that you want your account permanently deleted.

For security reasons, we may ask for additional information to verify that you own the account before we process the request.

3. What happens after deletion

Once a deletion request is completed, your account and associated user data are permanently deleted from the service, subject to any data we must retain for legal or accounting reasons.

If you use the in-app flow, deletion happens as part of that confirmed action. If you request deletion by email, we will process the request within a reasonable timeframe after verifying ownership of the account.

4. Related information

For more information about how we process personal data, see our Privacy Policy.
